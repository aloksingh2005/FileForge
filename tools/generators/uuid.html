About UUIDs

UUID (Universally Unique Identifier) is a 128-bit number used to uniquely identify information. This generator creates v4 UUIDs which are randomly generated.

Format: xxxxxxxx-xxxx-4xxx-yxxx-xxxxxxxxxxxx (where x is any hexadecimal digit and y is one of 8, 9, A, or B)
